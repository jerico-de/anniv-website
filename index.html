How It All Started

December 24, 2024 — The day we matched on Bumble. I remember seeing your profile and thinking, "Hahabol pa ba to Lord sa pasko? Bigay mo na siya sakin." I couldn't stop thinking about it to the point na I even shared it on my dump account. Para sure na makuha ko yung attention mo, I sent my very first message via email — tapos nag-message din ako sa Instagram hahahaha. Honestly thought na hindi na tayo maguusap ulit because of our first convo but THANK YOU PATRICE!

— the best swipe right I ever did ✨

gave it a chance

First Meet

December 29, 2024 — You said you wanted to hear my voice kaya nag-aya ka mag-call. During the call, naisip ko "What if mag-pretend ako na gutom ako and gusto ko mag koreanmart?" Buti nalang, nag-yes ko lumabas at around 2am. We spent around 4 hours together — simple lang, Mcdo to 711 — but it changed my life. Tapos siguro na-inlove ka na sakin kaya December 30, nag "friendly" date na tayo sa Trinoma, Vertis, and Centris.

— Mcdo to Mamou 💛

start of our journey

Something Changed

How did I realize I was in love? I found myself handcrafting 52 ribbon-made roses for you for Valentine's. Why 52? Kasi it's been 52 days since we met on Christmas Eve, up until Valentine's Day. Even before that, I always wanted to spend time with you and make you happy. But ever since I realized na I was in love with you, I wanted more of you, more of us.

— the moment I knew 🌹

✓ Tito Approved
step one

Asking Tito

Sinadya ko talaga na suggest si Tito para mag install ng sound system for studio. Yun yung naisip ko na way para matanong ko kung pwede kita ligawan and also, para may extra sideline si Tito. He answered yes kaagad kasi alam na daw niya na matanda ka na and capable of making your own decisions. Sabi niya din na bago sakanya yun kasi first time daw may nag ask sakanya kung pwede manligaw.

— the first yes ✨

✓ Team Cavite Approved
step two

Asking Tita & Cavite Family

Ito yung isa sa mga failed surprises ko. First thing I did was messaged Tita kung pwede ko ba siya makausap and said na hindi mo alam na nag-chat ako. Eh kaso sinend sa groupchat niyo hahaha. Althoug, medyo happy ako na ganon ang nangyare. Dahil dun, naging trip pa natin yung papuntang Cavite and bukod sa si Tita lang ang ma-meet ko, nameet ko din sila Tita Jhessa. So I'm happy with how it ended.

— the second yes 💛

✓ Lola Approved
step three

Asking Lola

I know how much you love Lola Myrna and how important she is to you. So asking permission from her is one of the most special permissions to get. Sabi ko pa noon "Hindi ako naniniwala sa mga gantong bagay" pero para sayo, gumawa ako ng paraan na sa tingin ko maririnig niya ako. I hope I'm making her happy and proud.

— lola's blessing 🌸
